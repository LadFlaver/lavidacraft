What is LavidaCraft?

LavidaCraft is a Minecraft texture pack that changes a small amount of textures to make the game look slightly better.

Features include:

Changes to netherite!

I always thought it was unfair that the new endgame gear didn't even look that good or flashy.

Changes to several blocks!

They look fancier.

Touches up some mobs!

They look a bit more realistic.

Improvements to UI!(default UI not shown)

Hearts more realistic and colors are more appealing to the eye.

And more!(not too much though)
