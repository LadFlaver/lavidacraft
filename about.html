What is the difference between LavidaCraft Official and Lavidacraft?

First of all if you even noticed this at all, I'm impressed. Lavidacraft was the first series of texture packs I made. The original idea was to change the textures I didn't like but soon I found that I had been changing many textures. Wanting to bring myself back to the original idea I decided to make Lavidacraft Favorites. It removed the low quality and low effort textures and only kept the ones I liked. I later decided to name the pack LavidaCraft Official, the name that remains today.
