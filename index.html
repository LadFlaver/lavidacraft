What is LavidaCraft?

LavidaCraft is a simple resource pack for Minecraft that improves and tweaks various parts of Minecraft while striving to preserve the Vanilla feel. It also features extensive configurability through the Respackopts mod. A Fresh Animations compatibility pack for LavidaCraft is available here.
